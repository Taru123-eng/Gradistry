Why Gradistry?

In today’s fast-paced academic world, keeping track of grades and understanding your GPA can be stressful and confusing. Gradistry was built to take the guesswork out of academic planning. With Gradistry, students can calculate, track, and plan their GPA with precision and ease.

Whether you’re taking regular courses, honors classes, or AP courses, Gradistry automatically handles weighted and unweighted GPAs so you can focus on learning rather than crunching numbers. Beyond calculation, Gradistry is designed with clarity and style in mind. Its intuitive interface, live course previews, and dynamic features like rotating testimonials make the process engaging and visually appealing. Gradistry is more than just a GPA calculator — it’s your academic companion. It helps students make informed decisions, track their performance, and stay motivated to achieve their goals.


Calculate. Track. Thrive. That’s Gradistry.
